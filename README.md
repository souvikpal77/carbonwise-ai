# CarbonWise AI 🌍

## Overview

CarbonWise AI is a sustainability-focused web application that helps users understand and reduce their carbon footprint. By providing simple carbon emission calculations and actionable recommendations, the platform encourages environmentally responsible decision-making and promotes climate awareness.

## Problem Statement

Climate change is one of the biggest global challenges today. Many people are unaware of how their daily activities, such as electricity usage, transportation, and consumption habits, contribute to carbon emissions. Existing tools can be complex and difficult for everyday users to understand.

CarbonWise AI addresses this problem by providing a simple and accessible platform that helps users estimate their carbon footprint and learn practical ways to reduce their environmental impact.

## Solution

CarbonWise AI enables users to:

* Calculate estimated carbon emissions.
* Understand the environmental impact of daily activities.
* Receive sustainability-focused recommendations.
* Build awareness about climate-conscious living.
* Take small but meaningful steps toward reducing emissions.
