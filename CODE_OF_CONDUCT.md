# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to creating a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

* Be respectful and professional.
* Welcome constructive feedback.
* Show empathy toward other contributors.
* Focus on collaboration and learning.

## Unacceptable Behavior

* Harassment or discrimination.
* Offensive comments or personal attacks.
* Any behavior that creates an unsafe environment.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove contributions or participation that violate this Code of Conduct.
